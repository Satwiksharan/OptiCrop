About OptiCrop

OptiCrop is a smart agricultural production optimization engine designed to bridge the gap between modern data science and everyday farming decisions.

Project goals

We aim to help farmers and agricultural planners identify the best crop choices by combining practical field conditions with predictive analytics.

Real impact

By improving crop selection and planning, the platform supports better yields, stronger resource efficiency, and more sustainable agricultural practices.

Technology stack

OptiCrop is powered by a Python Flask backend and a machine learning model that evaluates key environmental and soil indicators.
